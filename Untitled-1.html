About Our School

Founded in 1985, Sunshine Public School has been a beacon of excellence in education for over three decades. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and character development.

Our school follows a holistic approach to education, blending academic rigor with extracurricular activities to ensure the all-round development of every student. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we strive to create leaders of tomorrow.

Our Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Tour

Visit our campus or explore our virtual tour to experience our learning environment firsthand.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form below with all required documents.

3

Assessment

Students will participate in an age-appropriate assessment session.

4

Admission Decision

Receive notification of admission status within 10 business days.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school records (last 2 years)
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Medical/immunization records
  • Any special education plans (if applicable)
